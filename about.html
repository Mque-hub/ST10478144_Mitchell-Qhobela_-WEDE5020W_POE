About Us

Meet Mitchell Qhobela. The Photographer behind the lenses and artwork. Mitchell Founded Cathel Photography in 2020. Whilst in the middle of a life changing pandemic, the Corona Virus, He found his gift in art created by the lenses.
He started the business in a small town in Free State called Welkom. From 2021, he left his home town to gain the experience in Gauteng, Johannesburg. In his experience he has discovered that "what really matters is not about pressing a button to take the picture, it's about being able to understand the beauty of the moment. The emotions, the atmosphere, and the gift of the present moment."

At Cathel Photography, we love to see our clients happy and satisfied. Building a relationship with you is important to us.
